Some background on Unity:

Unity is most popular game engine in the world. It is used by most small and medium sized studios in New Zealand and around the world.

notable Unity games

narrative games: Firewatch, Gone Home, Kentucky Route Zero, 80 Days, some newer games by Tale of Tales (but not The Path)

NZ-made games: Mini Metro, Polybridge

Others: Kerbal Space Program, Hearthstone, Pokemon Go, Crossy Road, Angry Birds 2, Temple Run, Cuphead, more at https://unity3d.com/showcase/gallery
