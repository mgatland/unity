Learning Unity

Start by doing some of the official Unity tutorials. I recommend doing the Roll-a-Ball and Survival Shooter tutorials, then more if you have time.

Click here for details the official tutorials and notes on what is covered in each.

Starting your project

After you do two or three of the official Unity tutorials, you will be ready to start your own project. You should know enough Unity terms to be able to know what you don't know, and research it online.
